About Me

A Golfer Who Loves Teaching the Game

Hi, I'm Brooks. I'm a rising junior at Tufts University and a varsity college golfer. I grew up in Princeton and have been playing golf for most of my life.

I started Nassau Golf Co. to help players in the Princeton area get better in a relaxed, approachable way. As a college golfer, I can not only help with the swing but also advise on practice routines, course management, tournament prep, mindset, and what it takes to improve over time.

Whether you're brand new to golf, a junior trying to shoot lower scores, or someone who just wants to feel more comfortable on the course, I'll meet you where you are.

My lessons are simple, patient, and practical: no overcomplicated swing talk, no pressure, just clear coaching you can actually use on the course.
